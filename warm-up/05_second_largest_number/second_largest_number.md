### Find Second Largest Number

Write a function to find second largest number in an array.

**Example 1:**

> Input: nums = [1,8,2,9,7,0,3]
>
> Output: 8
>
> Explanation: Because 8 is the second largest number after the number 9

**Example 2:**

> Input: nums = [-2,-5,-6,-8,-1,-12]
>
> Ouput: -2
>
> Explanation: Because -2 is the second largest number after the number -1

**Example 3:**

> Input: nums = [10, 20, 8, 8, 3, 5, 20]
>
> Ouput: 10
>
> Explanation: Because 10 is the second largest number after the number 20
